Frequently Asked Questions

Sound is not working in the app?

Our app uses the built-in Text-to-Speech functionality in iOS. If you’re not hearing sound, please check the following settings:

  • Ensure your device is not on silent mode (check the mute switch on the side).
  • Turn up the volume using the hardware buttons.
  • Go to Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content and ensure “Speak Selection” or “Speak Screen” is enabled.
  • Make sure the system voice is downloaded: Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content > Voices, and tap a voice to download if needed.
  • Restart your device after changing any settings.
